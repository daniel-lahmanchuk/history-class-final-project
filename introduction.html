Superman's Introduction

Superman is a superhero character who was created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster during the beginning of WW2 and Hitler's Anti-Semitist tirade. He was created to represent America and its sense of justice.

Superman's origins tell a story of the ultimate immigrant who arrived on Earth from Planet Krypton after his parents saved him as a little child and put him in a rocket to Earth just in time just as the planet exploded.
